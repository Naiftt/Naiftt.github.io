About Me

My name is Naif Alkhunaizi, and I am a researcher in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I graduated with a Master's degree from Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence, the world's first AI university specializing in Machine Learning. Currently, I am working as a researcher, where I continue to develop my skills and knowledge in the field of AI. My research interests are primarily focused on Federated Learning, Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning, and Computer Vision. I am passionate about using AI to solve real-world problems and exploring the potential of AI in various fields, including healthcare.
